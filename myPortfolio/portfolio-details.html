About

This is a 3D software prototype designed for Sartex, a textile factory in Ksar Hlal, Monastir, to overcome the disadvantages of their previous 2D method. this new free prototype offers exceptional realism, a critical aspect in the presentation of the final product. The designer begins by choosing the jeans model. Next, they can modify the color of the model either by using the color palette or by selecting a predefined color. Finally, they choose a laser effect from the menu or create a new one, which is automatically applied to the jeans. By adopting this approach, Sartex will finally have its own software and will no longer be dependent on Jeanologia, enabling substantial cost savings.
