About

This game is still in progress. It's an inspiration of flappy bird but with a storyline and an objective. A bird called Moki goes out on an adventure to collect materials in order to rebuild his nest which was broken due to a heavy storm. He faces struggles in his journey but he tries to defy all the odds and show everyone that everything is possible. Currently, only the forest level is close to being finished. The other levels that are grayed in the world selection interface will be introduced in later versions of the game along with their corresponding stories. The backgrounds are generated by Leonardo AI and the assets are being imported from google and modified with photoshop. Big thanks to the designers Tarek Abbes and Anis Chaouachi for helping me with creating such astonishing assets.
